How to Play

Phase 1: Setup

  • Join a room with at least 4 players.
  • Players are split into Team 1 and Team 2.
  • Everyone receives 8 cards and must choose their best 5 to create the Game Deck.

Phase 2: The Rounds

There are 3 rounds. The same deck of cards is reused for each round!

Round 1: Charades

  • You can say anything EXCEPT the word on the card.
  • No skipping allowed!

Round 2: One Word

  • You can only say ONE WORD as a clue.
  • You may skip cards if stuck.

Round 3: Mime & Sounds

  • No words allowed! Only gestures and sound effects.
  • You may skip cards.

Winning

The team with the most points after Round 3 wins!
