Clue & Cue

Clue & Cue is a real-time multiplayer party game inspired by classic charades and word-guessing games. Players split into teams, select cards, and race against the clock to guess words based on clues, mimes, or single words depending on the round.


How to Play
- Lobby: One player creates a room and shares the Room Code (e.g., ABCD).
- Selection: All players receive a hand of 8 cards and must choose their best 5 to build the match deck.
- Gameplay:
	- Round 1: Charades / Unlimited Words (No skipping allowed!)
	- Round 2: One Word Only
	- Round 3: Mime & Sounds Only
- Winning: The team with the most points after 3 rounds wins!

Tech Stack
- Backend: Python (Flask)
- Real-time
- Communication: Flask-SocketIO (WebSockets)
- Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
- Deployment: Render (Gunicorn + Eventlet)
